About Us
Personalized Allergy Companion

PollenSense is your go-to mobile application for real-time allergy alerts and personalized management strategies. We are dedicated to making allergy management accessible and inclusive for everyone, with a particular focus on serving People of Color (POC) and promoting Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) in healthcare.

 

Say goodbye to sneezing fits and itchy eyes, and hello to enjoying the great outdoors without worry. With PollenSense, managing your seasonal allergies has never been easier or more tailored to your unique needs. Join our community today and take control of your allergies like never before!

Features
Personalized Allergy Alerts
Receive real-time notifications of potential allergens in your area based on local pollen forecasts.
Geolocation Integration
Automatically detects your location to provide pollen forecasts, and can input your location for travel purposes.
Data Analytics Dashboard
Track and analyze your allergy symptoms, and preventive measures over time to better understand and manage your allergies.
Accessibility Features
The app is accessible to users with disabilities with features such as screen reader compatibility, and adjustable contrast settings
Customized Strategies
Get personalized allergy prevention tips and treatment suggestions based on your specific health concerns and preferences.
Community Integration
Engage with friends, family, and fellow users in community forums to exchange tips, advice, and support.

What allergens does PollenSense track?
PollenSense tracks a wide range of allergens, including pollen from trees, grasses, weeds, as well as mold spores. Our platform utilizes advanced algorithms and data from reputable sources to provide accurate and up-to-date allergy alerts tailored to your specific location and allergen triggers.
